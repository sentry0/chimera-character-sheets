Secondary Attributes

Evasion

Determines how hard it is for you to be hit in combat by becoming the base difficulty required to hit you.

Equals your total Dodge die code x3 + pips

Resolve

Determines how hard it is for you to be hit in psychic combat by becoming the base difficulty required to hit you.

Equals your total Willpower die code x3 + pips

Soak

A component of how much physical damage you may endure. Subtracted from all incoming physical and energy damage.

Equals your total Stamina die code x3 + pips

Body Points (BP)

A pool which represents your health from which incoming damage is subtracted from (after Soak).

Equals your total Stamina die code x5 + pips

Character Points (CP)

May be spent on rolls to add an additional Wild Die to your pool. Also used to improve your characters capabilities.

Character points are gained at the end of an adventure

Fate Points (FP)

Spent before or after a die roll is made to double the amount rolled on the dice

Typically replenishes at the end of an adventure but may be awarded in game.
